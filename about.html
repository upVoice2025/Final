About Uplifting Voices

Mission Statement: Friendly online platform to involve all in the disability community who want to express their voices.

Personnel

Nupur Chokshi: Founder of Uplifting Voices. She has 18 years of experience working in the software industry. She has worked on various high-profile software development projects and web-based products as a project manager. She has ample experience working with clients as well as expertise in the integration of third-party products. As the founder of Uplifting Voices, she has made it her mission to provide people with disabilities their own online space to express their opinions. She has pledged to make a positive change in the life of every individual with disabilities.
